Senior Finance Specialist

Banking Sector I Startup I Executive Founder

As a Finance Specialist, you have the opportunity to join us where you can take on a wide range of responsibilities in a senior leadership role.

We welcome you to join our team if:
You get up and go to bed with Financial Analysis
You want to be a trusted member of our startup
Be an active participant in the business planning process
You are interested in analysing and interpreting financial data
You want to be involved in the financial reporting process and understand the financial data and its interpretations for effective decision making.

Your responsibilities will include:
Factoring Transaction Management:
structuring and managing transactions along the financial needs of clients
Structuring and managing transactions to meet customer needs, including documenting and monitoring transactions.
Developing Financial Strategy: you will be involved in the development and implementation of the company's long-term financial strategy, taking into account new technologies and market trends.
Legal and Regulatory Affairs: working with the legal and regulatory teams to obtain the necessary permits and comply with regulatory requirements.
Business Risk Analysis: assessing and managing business risks, including market risk, liquidity risk and other financial risks.
Prefer existing Open-banking system with practical application knowledge.

We are looking for you if:
- You have relevant experience in finance, preferably in banking
- Confident in speaking and writing English (advanced level)
- Open-minded and proactive with a problem-solving attitude
- Experience in fintech and factoring is an advantage

Salary:
We are looking for a founding co-leader. We are looking for a Founding Partner. Your salary will consist of profit and investment budget.
